a retreat on purpose

The 3 day retreat/conference is for senior corporate executives looking for an adventure - gaining new perspectives through hands on workshops, inspiring fireside keynotes, and reactivating creativity to maximize their role and company’s impact. Sometimes you need to get out of the office, set the refresh button, and realize the potential “purpose” has on elevating your business.


Throughout the 3 days you will learn how to effectively integrate purpose throughout your organization, communicate to all of your different stakeholders, and activate those audiences -- all while exploring and experiencing one of the most beautiful locations in the world.


Purpose plays a bigger role in business than ever before. Purpose can be the keystone of your brand story or the X-factor for retention and recruitment efforts. To effectively engage this dynamic challenge, we as business leaders must adapt. We need to challenge ourselves to be more creative and effective – with both our content and our methods of delivery.

Where

A luxury camp at the edge of Zion National Park, Utah, called Urban Canvas.

When

October 9 - 11,2018

Who attends:

The retreat is for senior corporate executives who have an interest or are involved with broadly defined brand/corporate purpose. This could be leaders within marketing, HR, Sustainability/CSR, public affairs, and communications departments.

    The Location

    Under Canvas® Zion

    Located just 45 min from St. George, Utah and steps outside of Zion National Park.

    Under Cnavas® Zion offers an extraordinary lodging experience just as fabulous as the park itself. This glamping camp in America's gorgeous Southwest is located on 196 acres bordering Zion National Park in Utah. Just moments away from incredible trails, off the beaten path adventures, and spectacular views of the majestic red rocks that make Zion so famous. The camp seamlessly blends into its dramatic surroundings and our luxury tents offer guests the opportunity to enjoy Utah's spectacular desert without giving up the comforts of home.

    How to get there:

    The best way to attend the retreat will be to fly into St. George Regional Airport(SGU). St. George Regional Airport has service to major hubs such as Salt Lake City, Los Angeles, Denver, and Phoenix. We will coordinate transportation for attendees to the retreat location, approximately a 30-45 min drive.

    Sustainability at Perspectives.

    Perspectives aims to mitigate its environmental impact. This year, we've set a challenge of diverting 100% of the waste generated by the conference. In collaboration with Waste Management, Terra Cycle, Natural Capital Partners, and Terracycle, we are striving to mitigate and minimize our event's environmental impact.
