    Get outside,
    activate purpose,
    and plan for impact.

    A three-day retreat for professionals looking to build purpose into their business

    October 9-11
    Zion National Park

    The three-day retreat is for professionals looking for an adventure –gaining new perspectives through hands-on workshops, inspiring fireside keynotes, and reactivating creativity to maximize their role and company’s impact. Sometimes you need to get out of the office, hit the refresh button, and realize the potential purpose has on elevating your business.


    Throughout the three days, you will learn how to effectively integrate purpose throughout your organization, communicate to all of your different stakeholders, and activate those audiences – all while exploring and experiencing one of the most beautiful locations in the world.


    In this hyper-connected and conscious economy, brands are actively looking to respond to consumers and employees calling on them to help better the world. Purpose can act as a North Star for brands navigating these uncharted waters; providing guidance on how to instill consumer trust and loyalty, retain top talent, and drive meaningful impact all while achieving long-term success.

    The Location

    Under Canvas® Zion

    Located just 45 min from St. George, Utah and steps outside of Zion National Park.

    Under Canvas® Zion offers an extraordinary lodging experience just as fabulous as the park itself. This glamping camp in America’s gorgeous Southwest is located on 196 acres bordering Zion National Park in Utah. Just moments away from incredible trails, off the beaten path adventures, and spectacular views of the majestic red rocks that make Zion so famous. The camp seamlessly blends into its dramatic surroundings and our luxury tents offer guests the opportunity to enjoy Utah’s spectacular desert without giving up the comforts of home.

    How to get there:

    The best way to attend the retreat will be to fly into St. George Regional Airport(SGU). St. George Regional Airport has service to major hubs such as Salt Lake City, Los Angeles, Denver, and Phoenix. We will coordinate transportation for attendees to the retreat location, approximately a 30-45 min drive.
