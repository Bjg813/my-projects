InsightOutside

a retreat on purpose

The 3 day retreat/conference is for senior corporate executives looking for an adventure - gaining new perspectives through hands on workshops, inspiring fireside keynotes, and reactivating creativity to maximize their role and company’s impact. Sometimes you need to get out of the office, set the refresh button, and realize the potential “purpose” has on elevating your business.


Throughout the 3 days you will learn how to effectively integrate purpose throughout your organization, communicate to all of your different stakeholders, and activate those audiences -- all while exploring and experiencing one of the most beautiful locations in the world.


Purpose plays a bigger role in business than ever before. Purpose can be the keystone of your brand story or the X-factor for retention and recruitment efforts. To effectively engage this dynamic challenge, we as business leaders must adapt. We need to challenge ourselves to be more creative and effective – with both our content and our methods of delivery.

Where

A luxury camp at the edge of Zion National Park, Utah, called Urban Canvas.

When

October 9 - 11,2018

Who attends:

The retreat is for senior corporate executives who have an interest or are involved with broadly defined brand/corporate purpose. This could be leaders within marketing, HR, Sustainability/CSR, public affairs, and communications departments.
